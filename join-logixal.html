Why Logixal

Logixal is associated with a 9 year old legacy and fortitude that comes from having endured tough times and emerging unscathed. What sets us apart is the clarity with which we define the roles and responsibilities, as well as the career progression of the individuals who join us. We hire the best minds in the industry and provide them with opportunities to discover their true potential and touch new heights in their career. We recognise exceptional performance, showcase the success of top performing teams, and reinforce a culture of nurturing future leaders of the company. We provide unique options to our employees including cross-functional careers, and co-location for peer-to-peer learning. We understand the value of productive collaboration among employees, which is in line with our 'one team' ideology. Our employees are also given the option to explore global assignments with Logixal entities, enhancing their global knowledge.

We are characterised by a fair and open evaluation system, which welcomes 360 degree feedback for leaders and two way feedback for junior staff. Our healthy and vibrant business culture focuses on the wellness of our employees.

We are committed to creating a diverse environment and are proud to be an equal opportunity employer. All qualified applicants receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, gender, gender identity or expression, national origin, genetics, disability, age or veteran status.
