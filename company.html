About us

Logixal is an application software services company specializing in eCommerce, eBanking and eInsurance domains. We provide omni channel solutions for Retail, Luxury Goods, Financial Services, Media, Publishing, Telecom and Subscription Model based businesses.

We are Partners in Excellence with enterprises seeking to enhance and transform their business models. Our focused investments in solution accelerators and evolving technologies help achieve superior business outcomes. The experience and proven capabilities of our talented team of over 100 professionals enables us to exceed customer expectations.

Our alliances with acknowledged world leading organizations ensure optimized solutions for our clients. Logixal is a Specialized Oracle Gold Partner. Our consultants are certified for their advanced competency in Oracle Commerce Products Suite (which includes Oracle ATG, Endeca and Web Center Sites) and Oracle FLEXCUBE, especially FLEXCUBE Direct Banking.

Our customers, spread across the United States, Europe, Middle East, Africa and Asia, are supported from our Offices in India, UK and the USA. In the last 9 years, we have a 100% track record of successfully extending and implementing product software for various enterprises. Logixal is known to deliver superior total cost of ownership (TCO) in Re-platforming/Upgrades as well as Greenfield implementation projects.

Our Approach

Our end goal at Logixal is to ensure our engagements provide best results in minimum time. To facilitate this goal, we adopt the best agile practices enabling us to provide challenging solutions with the best results and minimum amount of overhead and cost. We work on engagements ranging from long term structured engagements to ones that demand a war room environment with quick turnaround expectations.

Logixal operates with extreme transparency, providing complete visibility to the end clients about the status and progress of their projects. Logixal takes complete ownership of delivery and provides committed resources who work as One team with the client.

Our approach to transparency and ownership based delivery mirrors the fact that all our clients have chosen to continue long term, fruitful relationships with us.

We are a specialized partner of Oracle Corporation on Commerce Solutions, Content Management Solutions, Cloud, CRM and Financial products and platforms.

Our Oracle Partnership

We at Logixal, in partnership with Oracle, are focused on helping you achieve commerce excellence no matter where you are along the path.

As a Certified Specialist Oracle Commerce partner, we help retail companies with the following specialized services:

  • eCommerce Strategy: We can help you review your current commerce strategy, suggest a scale model for the future, and showcase how we can migrate your platform to Oracle ATG, ensuring existing features are fully implemented while helping leverage the more powerful features of Oracle ATG
  • Oracle Commerce Implementation and Upgrade: We have extensive implementation experience with all of Oracle ATG's commerce products, including Core Commerce, Endeca, Customer Service Center, Business Control Center
  • As a certified Specialist Flexucbe Direct Banking partners, we have engaged with customers to help them on their digital banking services. Our Accelerator packs have helped reduce the TCO considerably.

Leadership Team

Deepak Gala CEO

Deepak Gala | CEO

Deepak Gala is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Logixal. Deepak has over 18 Years of industry experience in driving and delivering mission critical system solutions for global clients in the areas of ecommerce and direct banking. Deepak's vast experience with Oracle Financial Services Software ranges from being the solutions architect to leading teams for product release efforts for their key internet banking relationships. His earlier work experience was with Wells Fargo Bank and Tata Consultancy Services.

Deepak drives the business strategy and focuses on building new markets. He strongly believes in partnering with clients to build a long term mutually beneficial relationship. He has a strong vision and an innate capability to conceptualize and visualize innovative solutions for clients.

Deepak holds a bachelor's degree from Mumbai University.

Uttam Gadkary President

Uttam Gadkary | Executive Vice President

Uttam Gadkary, is a senior London based professional and manages Logixal's business in Europe, Middle East and Asia (EMEA). In a career that spans over three decades, Uttam has shaped sales strategy, developed capabilities and built up teams in all the major markets. His work experience spans extremely successful organizations like Wipro, Citicorp Software, i-flex solutions and Oracle Financial Services Software, especially during their early entrepreneurial phases. Prior to joining Logixal, Uttam was Chief Operating Officer (COO) for Oracle Financial Services Software's European Subsidiary and VP of Global Sales Operations and Business Development.

Uttam's client driven perspective and global experience are core elements of his approach to managing both software product and service businesses. Building on his strong insights into the market place, he continues to be a stakeholder in the evolving onsite-offshore delivery model and understands how client organizations perceive risk and demand value. He is enthused by the potential for further innovation in business practices as well as in harnessing disruptive technologies to upgrade client business models.

He holds a Bachelor's degree in Engineering from College of Engineering, Pune and a Masters degree in Management from Jamnalal Bajaj Institute, Mumbai.
